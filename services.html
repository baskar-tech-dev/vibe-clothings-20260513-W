STEP-BY-STEP EXPLANATION

Deep Dive: Our 12 Manufacturing Steps

Explore the granular details of our world-class, premium apparel manufacturing lifecycle, ensuring absolute transparency and quality at every single touchpoint.

01
Phase 01 — Discovery & Alignment

Initial Consultation

We begin with an interactive, free discovery session to map out your product line vision. Our team aligns with your brand's aesthetic goals, target demographics, cost constraints, and shipping timelines to lay a robust roadmap.

  • Feasibility & cost structure analysis
  • Alignment on target market & fabric quality
  • Interactive design questionnaire walkthrough
02
Phase 02 — Blueprints & Technical Design

Sketches & Tech Pack Development

Turning creative mood boards into manufacturing blueprints. We detail everything from sizing guides, construction stitch lines, fabric specifications, prints, and trim bills of materials (BOM) in a comprehensive production-ready Tech Pack.

  • High-fidelity fashion sketches & flats
  • Multi-size measurement charts & grading setup
  • Component Bill of Materials (BOM) mapping
03
Phase 03 — Materials Procurement & Certification

Premium Fabric & Trim Sourcing

We tap into our extensive network of certified mill partners to source top-tier, high-durability fabrics and premium trims. From GOTS-certified organic cotton to custom metal buttons, we find exactly what matches your collection.

  • GOTS Certified 100% Organic Cotton & sustainable blends
  • Custom hardware, buttons, zippers, and wash-care labels
  • Lab-dip color matching (Pantone TPX/TCX)
04
Phase 04 — Dimensioning & Fit Mechanics

Master Pattern Making & Grading

Our master pattern makers translate 2D flat sketches into 3D structural patterns using advanced CAD drafting. We execute proportional grading across all standard size ranges, ensuring the exact same flattering fit in every size.

  • Precision CAD pattern drafting & nesting optimization
  • Consistent proportional sizing from XS to XXL+
  • Shrinkage & seam-allowance mathematical calculation
05
Phase 05 — Prototyping & First Physical Draft

Sample & Prototype Creation

Our dedicated sampling lab tailors the very first physical prototype of your garment. This prototype acts as the tangible proof-of-concept, allowing us to evaluate the real-world fabric drape, hand-feel, structure, and stitch strength.

  • Physical prototypes sewn by dedicated sample tailors
  • Direct fit evaluation on physical mannequins
  • Stitching and construction integrity validation
06
Phase 06 — Fit Assessment & Live Refinements

Fitting Session

We coordinate live fitting sessions with models to critique the prototype's dimensions, drape, and comfort. Whether analyzing neck drops, hem lengths, or pocket placements, we carefully evaluate the drape to ensure real-world comfort and functional wear.

  • Live fit-evaluation on professional fit models
  • Detailed assessment of ease, movement, and drape comfort
  • Precise measurement feedback loop and tech pack adjustments
07
Phase 07 — Perfection & Refinement

Revisions & Amendments

After live fit critiques, we revise pattern dimensions and refine construction specifications. This stage ensures all grading ratios are perfectly calibrated and approved before scheduling mass bulk production queues.

  • Nesting & CAD blueprint updates
  • Sizing ratio re-verification
  • Final pre-production tech pack lock
08
Phase 08 — Timeline & Resource Setup

Production Planning

Before machines start, our factory managers schedule active machinery queues, assign sewing operators, and perform comprehensive fabric consumption checks to minimize waste and secure critical bulk materials.

  • Machine queue allocation & material tracking
  • Streamlined assembly-line workflow design
  • Pre-production yield calculation for zero material waste
09
Phase 09 — Commercial Agreement & Costing

Agree Price & Quantity

With patterns, fabrics, and production plans locked in, we deliver a completely transparent, line-item commercial quote. We confirm bulk order quantities (MOQs), unit costs, payment terms, and absolute delivery deadlines.

  • All-inclusive unit cost transparency (no hidden charges)
  • Scalable volume discounts tailored to batch sizes
  • Legally binding delivery and payment commitments
10
Phase 10 — High-Volume Production & Assembly

Manufacturing

Your collection hits the main assembly floors. Using high-speed computerized cutting and industrial stitching machinery, our expert seamsters assemble your garments with remarkable precision, speed, and artisan-level consistency.

  • Industrial-scale cutting tables for absolute shape precision
  • Computerized stitching machinery with high tensile sewing thread
  • Real-time output monitoring for strict deadline compliance
11
Phase 11 — Validation & Standard Compliance

Inspection

We enforce a multi-stage Quality Assurance (QA) protocol. Every single garment is hand-inspected for stitching errors, loose threads, dimensional accuracy, button strength, and overall construction excellence.

  • Multi-point measurement checks against approved specs
  • Fabric tensile testing, button pull-tests, and colorfastness reviews
  • 100% finished product hand-inspections (AQL 2.5 standards)
12
Phase 12 — Dispatch & Global Logistics

Packaging & Delivery

The final leg of your journey. Garments are ironed, tagged, wrapped in biodegradable packaging, and securely boxed. We coordinate with elite courier partners to manage customs clearance and deliver door-to-door globally.

  • Customized, retail-ready premium unboxing setups
  • Hassle-free international customs, duties, and paperwork management
  • Fully-insured express air/ocean shipping with real-time tracking
