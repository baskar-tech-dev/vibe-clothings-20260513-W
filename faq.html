Production & Quality

How long does the manufacturing process take? ▼

Production timelines vary depending on the complexity of the design, order quantity, and customization requirements. Typically, lead times range from 4-8 weeks for standard orders. Rush orders may be available with additional costs. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the consultation phase.

What quality assurance processes do you have in place? ▼

We maintain 100% quality assurance through multiple stages:

  • Raw material inspection and testing
  • In-process quality checks during manufacturing
  • Final inspection before packaging
  • Compliance with international quality standards
  • Dedicated quality control team
What materials and fabrics do you work with? ▼

We work with a wide variety of premium materials including organic cotton, synthetic blends, performance fabrics, wool, silk, and sustainable eco-friendly materials. Our team can recommend the best fabric choices based on your product requirements, budget, and sustainability goals.

Ordering & Payment

What is the process to place an order? ▼

Our ordering process is straightforward:

  1. Contact us with your requirements via email or inquiry form
  2. Schedule a consultation to discuss your project in detail
  3. Receive a quotation with detailed pricing and timeline
  4. Finalize specifications and place the order
  5. Make payment and begin production
  6. Regular updates on production status
What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept multiple payment methods including bank transfers, wire transfers, letters of credit, and other arrangements. For detailed payment terms and conditions, please contact our team directly.

Is there a deposit requirement? ▼

Yes, we typically require a deposit to initiate production, with the balance due upon completion or as per agreed payment terms. The exact deposit amount will be discussed during the quotation phase and depends on the order size and requirements.

Shipping & Delivery

Do you handle shipping and logistics? ▼

We can assist with shipping arrangements or you can arrange your own logistics. We provide proper packaging to ensure your products arrive in perfect condition. We're experienced in international shipping to various destinations.

What are your shipping destinations? ▼

We ship to clients worldwide. Whether you're in Asia, Europe, Americas, or any other region, we have experience with international logistics and customs procedures. We'll work with you to find the most cost-effective and reliable shipping options.

Sustainability & Ethics

Absolutely. Sustainability is core to our operations.
